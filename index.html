About

We at SMTEH proudly introduce ourself as a premier education consultancy based in Bangalore, operating since 2014. We specialize in providing admission guidance in the fields of Medical, Engineering, Pharmacy, Nursing, B.Sc. Paramedical, Hotel Management and Many more, helping students secure seats in top colleges with whom we are associated. Over the past 10 years, we have successfully guided more than 2000+ students, ensuring satisfaction for both students and their parents. Our services cover everything from selecting reputed colleges, ensuring quality education, and facilitating exclusive placement opportunities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver accurate and comprehensive information to every student, helping them shine and build a bright future. We aim to guide them towards discovering their true passion and choosing the right career path. Choosing a college is one of life's most important decisions. At SMTEH, we guide students in such a way that they make informed decisions and never look back with regret. We are committed to provide the correct information and genuine guidance to help students select top institutes in Bangalore at an affordable cost. Our ultimate goal is to empower students to make wiser decisions and shine like a star.

Our Vision

With a strong focus on students careers, our approach is rooted in careful analysis of the educational market dynamics, industry requirements, college performance, and each students individual goals. Our vision is centered on serving students in the best possible way, providing them with the most suitable options and tailored career advice that leads to long-term success.

We finalize admissions physically at the college campus. After Completing pre-admission formalities, we request students and parents to visit Bangalore:

Our Services

At SMTEH, we follow a step-by-step approach for processing admissions:

  1. Initial Inquiry

    We request necessary documents from the student to verify eligibility.

  2. Document Verification & Counseling

    We provide:

    • A list of suitable colleges
    • Course details
    • Fee structures
    • Other essential information

  3. Pre-Admission Formalities

    Upon student confirmation, we help complete the pre-admission process.

  4. Final Admission at College

    We schedule a visit to Bangalore. Final admission is done in person for full transparency and satisfaction.
